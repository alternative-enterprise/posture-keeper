Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Posture Keeper (the "App") handles user information. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

Information We Collect

The App may collect the following information through third-party services.

Service Data Collected Purpose
Firebase Analytics App usage, screen transitions, crash reports App improvement and quality assurance
Google AdMob Device information, advertising ID, ad interaction data Ad delivery optimization

Information Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following information locally on your device only. This information is never transmitted to external servers.

  • Notification schedule settings (interval and time range)
  • Posture improvement tracking data
  • App preferences
  • Tutorial completion status
  • In-app purchase status

Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does not collect any of the following information.

  • Location data — No GPS or location data is collected.
  • Camera or microphone — The App does not access your camera or microphone.
  • Personal information — No personally identifiable information such as name, phone number, or email address is collected.
  • Health data — Posture improvement records stored on your device are never transmitted externally.

Third-Party Privacy Policies

You can review the privacy policies of third-party services used by the App at the following links.

  • Google (Firebase Analytics / AdMob)

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Retention and Deletion

Data stored on your device is completely deleted when you uninstall the App.

For data collected by third-party services, please refer to their respective privacy policies for information on data retention and deletion.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. If significant changes are made, we will notify you through the App or on this page.

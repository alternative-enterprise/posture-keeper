Posture Keeper

Improve Your Posture with Regular Reminders

About the App

Posture Keeper is a health care app that helps you improve your posture through regular notification reminders. With customizable notification schedules and posture improvement tracking, it helps you build a daily habit of better posture.

Frequently Asked Questions

I'm not receiving notifications

Please check that notifications are enabled for this app in your device settings. Power saving mode or battery optimization may also delay or stop notifications. Try reconfiguring the notification schedule in the app settings.

How do I change the notification interval?

You can freely customize the notification interval and time range from the Settings screen. Set it to match your daily routine, such as work hours or sleep time.

How can I remove ads?

You can remove ads through an in-app purchase. This option is available in the Settings screen.

How do I restore my purchase?

Tap the "Restore Purchases" button on the Settings screen. Your previous purchase will be restored as long as you are signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account.

What devices and OS versions are supported?

iOS 16 or later / Android 5.0 or later.
